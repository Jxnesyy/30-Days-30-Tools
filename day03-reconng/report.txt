Performed layered domain recon on tesla.com using Recon-ng framework in a dedicated workspace. Over 37 subdomains were discovered including marketing, VPN, gateway, and email systems. Resolved IPs indicate use of Akamai, Salesforce, and cloud-based infra. WHOIS contact modules exposed 10 Tesla employee names and email addresses. This confirms the power of structured modular OSINT workflows in identifying both technical surfaces and human targets. Suitable for red team prep or exposure audit reports.
